Itinerary & operations notice

This travel program is operated using the itinerary and destinations planned at present to deliver the best possible experience. However, parts of the schedule, places visited, or program content may change due to unforeseen external factors—including flight schedules, local weather, transport and operating conditions, or circumstances at institutions and partner facilities.

Should such changes occur, our principle is to provide substitute itineraries and programs of equal or higher quality so that the overall quality of participants’ travel experience is maintained.

We also aim to inform participants of any changes in advance whenever possible. Even when immediate adjustments are necessary on site, we will do our utmost to proceed with sufficient explanation for all participants.

This notice supports stable and smooth program operation. We kindly ask for your understanding and cooperation.
