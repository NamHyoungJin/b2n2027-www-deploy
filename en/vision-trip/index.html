Vision Trip participation notice

Vision Trip schedules are divided into before the B2N event (Before) and after (After).

Each Vision Trip group is planned for a minimum of 40 participants; numbers may vary depending on enrollment and special circumstances, and if there are changes, the confirmed participation fee will be announced before the balance payment deadline.

Each group may select only one Vision Trip course.

Scheduling prioritizes group-based travel; individual or small-group applications may join another group or proceed jointly with other small-group trip participants.

A professional guide is assigned per group and course. Hotel accommodation is based on double occupancy (two per room); additional charges apply for single-room use.

The Vision Trip cannot be offered on its own without participating in the B2N event, and individual or team-only Vision Trips after the B2N event are also not available.

Tour arrangements in Korea are provided through Hana Tour; by applying, you are deemed to have agreed to Hana Tour’s terms of use.

Further details and orientation schedules will be communicated separately to individual participants or to each trip group’s leader.
