About B2N | About

In April 2027, this global arts and cultural exchange takes place in Antalya, Türkiye, at Aspendos, a world-renowned heritage ancient theatre. It is conceived as an international cultural platform where participants from many countries gather in one place to connect through performances, hands-on experiences, business, networking, and cultural exchange programs.

B2N aims to be a new kind of global cultural gathering where tradition and modernity, art and technology, and culture and industry come together. It is organized as a large-scale international event joined by professionals across fields—including artists, planners, companies, institutions, and organizations—with the goals of expanding the cultural industries and building sustainable global networks.

Through close collaboration with institutions in Türkiye and international partners, we seek to promote cultural tourism and strengthen cultural exchange between regions and nations. We will continue to grow as a central platform connecting Korea and the world.

Participants will experience high-quality programming in historic spaces and find opportunities for diverse cooperation and exchange across countries and fields. We invite you to an international festival where we enjoy culture together, pray for the restoration of the nations, and share hearts of blessing.
