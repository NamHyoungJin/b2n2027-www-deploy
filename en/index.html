About B2N | About

In April 2027, in Antalya, Türkiye, at the world heritage Aspendos ancient theatre, B2N brings together participants from many countries for performances, exhibitions, networking, and cultural programs.

The event aims at a new kind of global cultural festival where tradition and modernity, art and technology, culture and industry meet. Participants experience rich programming in historic spaces and build cooperation across borders.

Creators, planners, companies, institutions, and organizations join in a large-scale international format—expanding cultural industry and sustainable global networks.

Together with local institutions and international partners, we support cultural tourism and exchange—and grow as a hub connecting Korea and the world.

In April 2027, in Antalya and Aspendos, we invite the nations to enjoy culture together, pray for restoration, and share blessing.
